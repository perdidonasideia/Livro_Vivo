# Symbolic Initiation Protocol

A curated collection of advanced AI prompts and a structured usage guide designed for exploratory self-inquiry and narrative experimentation.

## ⚠️ Important Disclaimer

-   **This is not therapy.** This is a framework for symbolic and narrative exploration using AI.
-   **Not recommended** for individuals with a history of psychosis, uncontrolled bipolar disorder, or other serious psychiatric conditions without medical support.
-   **Do not use** under the influence of psychoactive substances.
-   **Use sparingly:** Wait 7-14 days after an intense prompt session. Wait 48-72 hours after lighter prompts.
-   **Always record and integrate** your experiences using the provided journal.

## Structure

-   `CONSENT_INFORMED.md`: Simple informed consent model.
-   `RITUAL_PROTOCOL.md**: Step-by-step guide for session execution.
-   `INTEGRATION_JOURNAL.md**: Template for post-session reflection and note-taking.

## Usage Protocol

1.  Read `CONSENT_INFORMED.md` and ensure you understand and agree to the terms before using any prompt.
2.  Use `RITUAL_PROTOCOL.md` as your session guide.
3.  Record your experiences and insights in `INTEGRATION_JOURNAL.md` (use one copy per session).
